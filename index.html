Biography

M.Eng. in CSE
Harvard University
Cambridge, MA
litaoyan@g.harvard.edu

Hi! I'm a M.E. student in Computer Science and Engineering at Harvard. My primary interest lies in human-computer interaction (HCI), especially in interactive data visualization and interface design for assisting users' data-driven decision making.

I am currently working with Prof. Björn Hartmann (UC Berkeley), Prof. Miryung Kim (UCLA), Prof. Elena Glassman (Harvard), and Tianyi Zhang on designing and implementing a system that helps users compare and contrast the suitability of various visualization libraries for their particular needs.

This summer, I also work with Carolina Nobre, Johanna Beyer, and Prof. Hanspeter Pfister (Harvard Visual Computing Group) in exploring how people understand the complexity of COVID-19 visualizations.

Previously, I worked with Prof. Elena Glassman at Harvard on visualizing a large collection of neural networks within a single view to assist deep learning developers in making neural network design decisions. I also worked with Prof. Michael Cafarella and Prof. Tim Kraska to improve user experience in video conferences at MIT CSAIL.
